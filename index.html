I am an Associate Consultant — Data Scientist at Nomura Research Institute (NRI), Gurgaon, a leading Japanese IT and management consulting firm serving financial and enterprise clients globally. I hold an MS in Applied Statistics and Informatics from IIT Bombay.

My work spans the full data science lifecycle — from exploratory analysis and statistical modelling to deploying production ML systems. I build agentic AI pipelines with LangChain and LangGraph, large-scale NLP systems, and forecasting models.
